Title:
UCM-T chem / VSEPR sphere v.1: Minimal sphere model for emergent molecular-like geometries

Upload type:

Publication type:
Report

Description:
This release contains the v.1 article and reproducibility bundle for the UCM-T chem / VSEPR sphere module. The work studies whether familiar molecular-like geometries can emerge from a minimal model on the sphere using only two node types (B and L), repulsive interactions, a sphere constraint, and energy minimization. The bundle includes the paper source and PDF, publication figures, representative JSON outputs, the full N=6 map series used for the soft octahedral summary figure, and the scripts used for generation and postprocessing.
